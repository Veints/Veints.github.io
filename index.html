Racero

The New .io game!

Racero is a 1-2 player game. In the original game mode, you play as a block trying to dodge Purple squares, while aiming to collect the yellow square. As you continue to collect the yellow square, the arena gets smaller and smaller. Until it is so small you hit a Purple square.
